About Me

Hello my name is Gabe Oldenhof. These are the experiences that are most defining to me. I have graded each of my experiences on the criteria of how much they have impacted myself positively. Additionally there will be a section dedicated to my future in terms the goals I have in various aspects of my life.

Past Experiences

Case Competition

Winning the Laurier Case Competition in March 2024. What was so good about it was the sheer will and determination it took to come up with a grand and professional solution. My favorite memory was staying up for the majority of the night and putting my all into the work. Overall grade: A.

High School Graduation

Graduating high school in June 2023 was a bittersweet moment. The best part was moving on to new opportunities, but I miss the friendships I formed. It was truly a surreal moment in time, one that is unforgettable Favorite memory: throwing our grad caps in the air. Overall grade: B.

Ontario Baseball Championship

Playing with the Aurora Jays in 2016 was unforgettable. Winning the championship game and celebrating with teammates created memories I’ll cherish forever. Overall grade: A.

University Move-In

Moving into Expositor Place at Wilfrid Laurier University in September 2024 was my first taste of independence. Meeting new friends made it memorable, despite feeling homesick. Overall grade: B.

G2 Driver's License

Passing my G2 driver's license test in February 2022 was an exciting milestone. It gave me the freedom to drive on my own. Favorite memory: hearing “you passed.” Overall grade: A.
